Welcome

Welcome to Animal Hospital of Melbourne

For more than 30 years, our hospital has been part of the Melbourne community, providing high quality veterinary medicine with the warmth of a neighborhood practice. Our clients aren't just clients, they're family.

Whether you're new to the area, welcoming a new four legged family member, or simply looking for an experienced vet who treats you both with respect and compassion, we'd love to meet you.

Have a question right now? Pawsy, our chat assistant in the corner, can answer questions and help you request an appointment any time, day or night.

About Us

Meet our doctors

Sample team profiles for this demo. Your real doctors, photos, and bios drop straight in here.

👩‍⚕️

Dr. Amelia Santos

Co-Owner, Veterinarian

Dr. Santos grew up on a family farm surrounded by dogs, goats, and one very opinionated parrot, so a life in veterinary medicine always felt inevitable. After earning her veterinary degree, she spent a decade in companion animal practice before becoming Co-Owner here in Melbourne.

She has particular interests in wellness care, preventative medicine, and client education, and she's happiest when a nervous pet leaves the exam room with a wagging tail.

At home: two rescue Labradors, Biscuit and Waffle
👨‍⚕️

Dr. Marcus Bell

Veterinarian, Surgery & Dentistry

Dr. Bell found his calling during a college volunteer shift at an animal shelter, where he discovered that surgery combined the precision he loved with the outcomes that mattered most. He completed advanced training in soft tissue surgery and veterinary dentistry.

Patients know him for his calm hands, and their owners know him for explaining every procedure in plain language before it happens.

At home: a senior Boston Terrier named Pepper
👩‍⚕️

Dr. Priya Nair

Associate Veterinarian

Dr. Nair is our resident cat whisperer and senior pet advocate. After starting her career in emergency medicine, she moved to general practice to build the long term relationships with families that she loves most about this work.

She focuses on feline medicine, senior wellness plans, and helping pets age comfortably, and she's a firm believer that treats fix almost any exam room mood.

At home: three cats who supervise everything she does
